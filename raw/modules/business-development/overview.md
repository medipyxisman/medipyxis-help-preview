# Business Development overview

The Business Development dashboard centralizes your team's outreach activity, spend tracking, and AI-generated route suggestions in a single screen.

![Business Development dashboard showing widgets and bottom action bar](../../assets/bd/dashboard.png)

*Business Development dashboard. Widgets occupy the main canvas; the bottom action bar provides one-tap shortcuts to the four most common tasks.*

## Dashboard widgets

| Widget | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Monthly Spend Reports** | Summarizes marketing and entertainment spend for the current month by rep and region. Links to the full spend log. |
| **New Patients Queue** | Lists recently converted referrals attributed to BD outreach, giving reps visibility into pipeline outcomes. |
| **Next Best Action** | AI-generated prompt recommending the highest-priority contact or account to engage today, based on last-contact date, referral volume trend, and spend history. |
| **AI Route Suggestions** | Proposes an optimized daily visit sequence for the rep. See [AI Route Suggestions](./ai-route-suggestions.md) for the full methodology. |
| **Today's Route** | The confirmed route for today — finalized after the rep accepts or edits the AI suggestion. |

## Bottom action bar

Four shortcuts at the bottom of the screen launch the most common BD workflows:

| Action | What it opens |
|---|---|
| **Log Call** | Phone call logging form — practice, contact, call outcome, next steps. |
| **Add Spend** | Spend entry form — category, amount, associated practice, receipt upload. |
| **New Visit** | In-person visit log form — date, practice, contact, visit notes. |
| **New Contact** | CRM new-contact form — adds a physician, office manager, or referral coordinator to an existing practice record. |
