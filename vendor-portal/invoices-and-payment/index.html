Track invoices and payment in Accounts Payable

how-to ⏱ 6 min read Vendor Updated 2026-04-24

Monitor the invoice lifecycle for your products from the moment a shipment is received through payment confirmation.

Before you start

  • You need vendor_coordinator role.
  • Invoices are generated by the practice's finance team, not by you. Your role is to review and dispute, not create.
  • Ensure your shipments have been received correctly (see Shipments and UINs) — invoices reference the received UINs.

Accounts Payable columns

Navigate to Inventory → Accounts Payable to see the invoice list for your products.

Column Description
VENDOR Your company name
INVOICE # The invoice number the practice assigned
DATE Invoice issue date
DUE Payment due date
TOTAL Total amount due for this invoice
STATUS Current payment status (see below)
ACTIONS Available actions for this invoice row

Invoice statuses

Status Meaning
Draft Invoice created but not yet sent to you for review
Pending Invoice sent; awaiting payment processing
Approved Practice has approved the invoice for payment
Paid Payment has been issued
Disputed You or the practice has flagged a discrepancy
Overdue Payment due date has passed without payment

Review an invoice

  1. Navigate to Inventory → Accounts Payable.
  2. Use the VENDOR filter to show only your company's invoices.
  3. Click any invoice row to open the detail panel.
  4. The detail panel shows: - Line items: each product unit (by UIN), quantity, unit price, and extended amount. - Payment terms. - A PDF download of the invoice document.
  5. Verify the line items match what you shipped and received.

Dispute an invoice

If the invoice contains an error (wrong quantity, wrong price, or a unit you did not ship):

  1. Open the invoice detail panel.
  2. Click Dispute.
  3. Select a dispute reason from the dropdown: Incorrect Quantity, Wrong Unit Price, Product Not Shipped, Duplicate Invoice, Other.
  4. Enter a note explaining the discrepancy.
  5. Click Submit Dispute.

The invoice status changes to Disputed. The practice's finance_ap user receives a notification and will contact you to resolve. Disputed invoices are excluded from the overdue calculation until resolved.

The dispute workflow is a communication channel — it does not automatically issue credits or adjustments. Resolution happens between your AP contact and the practice's finance team outside Medipyxis, with the outcome recorded back in the invoice notes.

Payment confirmation

When a payment is issued, the practice's finance team updates the invoice status to Paid and may attach a remittance document. You will receive a notification.

To confirm payment details: 1. Open the invoice from Accounts Payable. 2. Review the Payment Date and Remittance attachment in the detail panel. 3. Reconcile against your own accounts receivable records.

Invoice summary view

The Accounts Payable list supports filtering and sorting by all columns. Use these combinations to monitor your portfolio:

Filter combination Use case
STATUS = Pending Outstanding invoices awaiting payment
STATUS = Overdue Escalation candidates
STATUS = Disputed Active disputes requiring follow-up
DUE ascending Oldest due dates first

Troubleshooting

Symptom Likely cause What to do
Invoice does not appear in your list Shipment not confirmed as received Confirm receipt in Inventory → Receiving
Invoice total does not match your records Price discrepancy or incorrect quantity File a dispute
Dispute not resolving Practice finance team not responsive Escalate to the practice admin
Invoice stuck in Draft Practice has not finalized the invoice Contact the practice's vendor_coordinator
